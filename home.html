Projects



Air Quality Monitering System


Within our IoT-based Air Quality Monitoring project, we've integrated the MQ135 and LM35 sensors, each responsible for detecting harmful gases and providing voltage outputs corresponding to their concentrations. The Arduino UNO microcontroller acts as the central processing unit, translating these readings into PPM (Parts Per Million) units for comprehensive air quality assessment, which is conveniently displayed on an LCD screen.
